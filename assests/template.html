Lively Wallpaper

Lively Wallpaper is a powerful open-source application that transforms your static desktop into a dynamic, interactive canvas. It supports a wide variety of animated wallpapers, including high-resolution videos, GIFs, HTML web pages, and real-time 3D simulations. The software is highly optimized to automatically pause playback when full-screen applications or games are running, ensuring zero performance impact on your system. Users can customize every aspect of their wallpapers, from speed and brightness to audio reactivity, while enjoying full support for multi-monitor setups. It’s the perfect tool to bring your workspace to life without sacrificing speed.
