Lively Wallpaper

Lively Wallpaper is a powerful open-source application that transforms your static desktop into a dynamic, interactive canvas. It supports a wide variety of animated wallpapers, including videos, GIFs, web pages, and real-time 3D simulations. The software is highly optimized to pause playback when full-screen applications or games are running, ensuring zero performance impact on your system. Users can customize every aspect of their wallpapers, from speed and brightness to audio reactivity. With support for multi-monitor setups and ultra-wide resolutions, it brings your workspace to life. Lively also includes a built-in creator tool, allowing you to turn your own media files into stunning desktop backgrounds instantly.
