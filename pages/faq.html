Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Getnaro services and apps.

What is Getnaro?
Getnaro is a clean, ad-free, safe download hub that redirects users to official software and driver websites.
What type of downloads do you offer?
Getnaro provides Windows apps, PC tools, drivers, utilities, and essential software.
Are downloads safe?
Yes. Every download redirects to the official website, guaranteeing authenticity and virus-free software.
Do you host files?
No. Getnaro never hosts files. We are like "courier boys" — we deliver you to the official website.
Do I need an account?
No. There are no accounts, no logins, and no sign-ups to download files. Everything is open.
Does Getnaro collect personal data?
No user data, no browsing data, no analytics collection — 100% privacy.
Are there ads?
No. There are zero ads and zero promotions on our platform.
Is anything paid?
No. Getnaro does not sell downloads, subscriptions, or premium tiers.
How do you ensure safety?
We never serve files ourselves. All links point directly to the official developer site, ensuring you get the original file.
How often are links updated?
We update links frequently based on official developer update cycles.
Are you partnered with any companies?
No. Getnaro has no affiliation or partnership with any brand. We are an independent directory.
What support do you provide?
Basic support is available Monday–Friday on eligible business days via email or Discord.
What is your response time?
Average response time is typically 24–36 hours.
Do you help with installation issues?
We offer general troubleshooting but cannot provide deep technical debugging for third-party software.
Is the app available for mobile?
Currently only for Windows. Linux and Android versions are planned for late 2026.
Does the website work on all browsers?
Yes, Getnaro works smoothly on all modern browsers (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Brave, Opera).
What if a download link is broken?
Just contact us on Instagram, Discord, or WhatsApp using the Contact page. We fix links quickly.
Do you own any software listed?
No. All software belongs to their respective companies and developers.
Can I redistribute downloaded files?
No. Redistribution is not allowed since files come from official sources, you should share the official link instead.
What if a downloaded file doesn’t work?
Please check the official developer site for support — they host the file, not us.
Do you provide installation guides?
We are planning simplified guides, but official installation instructions from the developer are recommended.
Can users request new apps?
Yes, you can request apps or drivers through our official social channels or the Contact form.
What features does the Getnaro app have?
Fast official-download redirection, zero ads, no tracking, simple UI, and auto-installation features.
Will new updates come?
Yes — Getnaro is continuously evolving with UI and feature improvements regularly.
Where can I follow your updates?
You can follow us on Instagram, Discord, Reddit, WhatsApp, and YouTube. Links are in the footer.
