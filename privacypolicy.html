Privacy Policy

Getnaro respects your privacy. We operate with a strict no-tracking, no-data-collection philosophy.

1. No Data Collection

We do not collect personal data, browser data, analytics, cookies, or device identifiers.

2. No Accounts

Getnaro does not require or support user accounts, login systems, or any form of registration.

3. No Hosting of Files

Getnaro only redirects users to official websites for downloading apps, tools, drivers, and software.

4. Third-Party Websites

All downloads are hosted by official developers. Their own privacy policies may apply once you leave Getnaro.

5. No Advertising

We do not run ads, popups, banners, trackers, or affiliate marketing scripts.

6. Security

Because downloads come directly from official developers, security is governed by those sources.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may revise this privacy policy if needed. Changes will reflect instantly on this page.
